Creamy Spinach and Corn Maggie

Ingredients

  • 1 packet maggie
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic
  • 0.5 cup corn kernels
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 0.5 cup milk
  • 0.5 cup water
  • 1 bread slice
  • 1 teaspoon butter

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil and fry the minced garlic for a minute.
  2. Add the corn and fry for another minute.
  3. Add spinach and sauté for 2 minutes.
  4. Cook MAGGI Masala Noodles with ½ cup milk and 1 cup water.
  5. To make the breadcrumbs, crumble the slice of bread and sauté in butter till golden-brown.
  6. Pour the MAGGI Noodles on a plate, and top it with the sautéed spinach. Garnish with toasted breadcrumbs. Serve hot.
